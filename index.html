About Me

I bring over two decades of experience in economic development, strategic communications, and public-private partnerships. My work has included federal grant acquisition and a $30M infrastructure project that shaped how I think about systems, stakeholders, and long-term impact.

I'm currently a graduate student building design and web development skills — bridging analytical work with visual and interactive storytelling. I'm interested in how clear design makes complex information accessible to the people it's meant to serve.

Hobbies & Interests

Reading & Research

Deep interest in 20th-century political and social theory, media literacy, and the intersection of communication and power.

Writing

Long-form nonfiction and memoir work, with long-term goals in comedy writing and performance.

Life at Home

Living in Mount Vernon with two dogs who remind me daily that rest and play belong in a serious life.

Career Goals

My goal is to combine strategic communications experience with design and web skills to support organizations doing meaningful public-interest work. I'm particularly interested in freelance and independent projects that let me write, design, and build across the full arc of a communication challenge.
